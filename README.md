Worldwide English with Mr. Addis

Welcome to the Worldwide English with Mr. Addis website project.

About

Worldwide English with Mr. Addis is an online learning platform created to help students improve their English language skills through clear, practical, and engaging lessons.

The website provides:

- English lessons for Grades 4–8
- Grammar tutorials
- Vocabulary practice
- Reading and writing activities
- Downloadable worksheets
- Online quizzes
- Video lessons
- Online tutoring information

Mission

To make quality English education accessible to students through simple, effective, and engaging online learning resources.

Features

- Responsive design for mobile and desktop

- Contact page
